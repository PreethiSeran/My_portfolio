01 · About

Two disciplines, one instinct.

I graduated in 2026 with a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science from C.K. College of Engineering and Technology, Anna University — but the real throughline in my work isn't the degree, it's a habit of mind: I notice when something doesn't add up, and I care about how that gets communicated.

On the engineering side, that's meant building a Zero Trust access-control system with real-time anomaly detection, and training classifiers that catch fake accounts and predict customer churn before either becomes a problem. On the design side, it's meant sitting with Figma until a flow feels obvious instead of clever — case in point, a full brand identity and ordering experience for a coffee & matcha label called Still Brew.

I'm looking for a team that needs both halves of that — someone who can reason about a model's confidence score and also design the screen that explains it to a user who's never heard of an Isolation Forest.
